MEET AND GREET

We always start our design process with a sit down meeting with our clients, whether it’s in person, over the phone or via Skype. During these meetings we discuss your general design needs, vision, as well as pricing.

QUOTE

Next, we will present you with a custom quote for your project. This is based off of what was discussed during the initial meeting.

CONTRACT AND DEPOSIT

Once the quote is approved, we ask that you sign a contract for the work to be done. We also require a $150 deposit before any design work begins.

FIRST PROOF

The timeline for the proofing process is determined by the dates of your event and your personal needs. For the initial rounds of proofs, you will receive a digital copy of your design work. We will then make changes based on your feedback.

FINAL PROOF

Once all revisions are made, we will send you a hard copy proof for your signed approval. Here is where we ask that you double-check spelling and all information. After this approval, any changes that must be made will require an additional fee.

PRINTING AND ASSEMBLY

Following final approval of the design, we will have your product printed. We can also assemble your invites for an additional fee. Or, we can ship everything assembly-ready.

DELIVERY!

Once final payment is received, your order will be shipped right to your doorstep! For local customers, we also offer personal delivery.
